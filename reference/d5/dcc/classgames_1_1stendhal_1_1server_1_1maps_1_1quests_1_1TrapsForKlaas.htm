Detailed Description

QUEST: Traps for Klaas

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Klaas (the Seaman that takes care of Athor's ferry's cargo)

STEPS:

  • Klaas asks you to bring him rodent traps.

REWARD:

  • 1000 XP
  • 5 greater antidote
  • note to apothecary (if Antivenom Ring quest not started)
  • Karma: 10

REPETITIONS:

  • Every 24 hours
