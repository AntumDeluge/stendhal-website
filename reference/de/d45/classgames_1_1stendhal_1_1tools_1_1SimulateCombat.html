Detailed Description

Class for simulating player vs. creature combat.

Note: some of the following code is taken from games.stendhal.tools.BalanceRPGame

Usage: games.stendhal.tools.SimulateCombat –lvl <lvl> –hp <hp> –atk <atk> –def <def>[ –rounds <rounds>] games.stendhal.tools.SimulateCombat –help

Parameters
--lvlLevel at which player & enemy should be set.
--hpHP value of enemy.
--atkAttack level of enemy.
--defDefense level of enemy.
--roundsNumber of rounds to simulate (default: 500).
--thresholdDifference threshold used to determine if combat was balanced.
--creatureUse a predefined creature as enemy.
--barehandedEntities will not be equipped with weapons & armor.
--equipsameEnemy will be equipped with same weapons & armor as player.
--noboostPlayer will not get boost from equipment.
--fairGives player weapon with atk 5 & no other equipment (overrides –barehanded & assumes –noboost).
--bossDenotes enemy is boss type (currently doesn't affect anything).
--allRuns simulation for each predefined creature.
--helpShow usage information & exit.
