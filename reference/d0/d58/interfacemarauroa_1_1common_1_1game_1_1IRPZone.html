Detailed Description

Interface for managing the objects in a RPZone. An RPZone is a storage for objects, that has an unique per World associated id. It must provide at least methods for: * adding * removing * querying * modifying

It almost provide methods that are called on load and on unload of the zone. And finally it must provide Perception when asked by
