Detailed Description

The behaviour of an NPC who is able to produce one or more things for a player, given that the player brings the necessary resources. Production takes time, depending on the amount of the product requested.

◆ questSlot

final String games.stendhal.server.entity.npc.behaviour.impl.MultiProducerBehaviour.questSlot
private

To store the current status of a production order, each MultiProducerBehaviour needs to have an exclusive quest slot.

This slot can have three states:

  • unset: if the player has never asked the NPC to produce anything.
  • done: if the player's last order has been processed.
  • number;product;time: if the player has given an order and has not yet retrieved the product. number is the amount of products that the player will get, product is the name of the ordered product, and time is the time when the order was given, in milliseconds since the epoch.
