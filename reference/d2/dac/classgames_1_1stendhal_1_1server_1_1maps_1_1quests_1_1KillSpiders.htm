Detailed Description

QUEST: Kill Spiders

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Morgrin

STEPS:

  • Groundskeeper Morgrin ask you to clean up the school basement
  • You go kill the spiders in the basement and you get the reward from Morgrin

REWARD:

  • magical egg
  • 5000 XP
  • 11 karma in total

REPETITIONS:

  • after 7 days.
