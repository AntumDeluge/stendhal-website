This function should be overwritten by all classes that want to modify an PCM audio stream. The audio data is uniform and interleaved. uniform: Each sample has a value between -1.0 and 1.0 interleaved: The channels are not separated. They are bundled in frames e.g. if there is stereo PCM data: data[0] and data[1] are the left and right channels of sample 0 data[2] and data[3] are the left and right channels of sample 1 data[4] and data[5] are the left and right channels of sample 2 and so on ...

The number of samples can be calculated by: frames * channels

Parameters
datathe audio data
framesthe number of sample frames contained in "data"
channelsnumber of channels
ratethe sample rate
