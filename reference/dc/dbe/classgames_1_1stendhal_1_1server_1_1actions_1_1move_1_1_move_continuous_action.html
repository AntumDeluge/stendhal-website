Detailed Description

Action that allows a player to continue movement after teleport via portal or after map change without the need to release and press direction again.

To enable/disable:

  • Execute slash command "movecont".
  • Change "Continuous Movement" setting in settings dialog.

FIXME: Causes issues with portals where player is teleported to side opposite of direction pushed.
