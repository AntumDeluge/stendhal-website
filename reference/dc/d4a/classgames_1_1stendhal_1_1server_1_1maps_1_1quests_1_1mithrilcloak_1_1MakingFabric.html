void games.stendhal.server.maps.quests.mithrilcloak.MakingFabric.makeThreadStep ( )
private

Tries to take all the resources required to produce the agreed amount of the product from the player. If this is possible, initiates an order.

Parameters
npcthe involved NPC
playerthe involved player

This method is called when the player returns to pick up the finished product. It checks if the NPC is already done with the order. If that is the case, the player is told to get the product from another NPC. Otherwise, the NPC asks the player to come back later.

Parameters
npcThe producing NPC
playerThe player who wants to fetch the product
