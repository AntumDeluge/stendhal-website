Detailed Description

A utility for cleaning whitespace in files.

Usage: CleanWhitespace <dir>[ <dir> ...] <ext>[,<ext>,...]

Parameters
dirDirectory or list of directories to scan.
extComma-separated string of filename extensions to include in scan.
