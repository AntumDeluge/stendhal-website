Detailed Description

QUEST: Take gold for Grafindle

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Grafindle
  • Lorithien

STEPS:

  • Talk with Grafindle to activate the quest.
  • Talk with Lorithien for the money.
  • Return the gold bars to Grafindle

REWARD:

  • 200 XP
  • some karma (10)
  • key to nalwor bank customer room

REPETITIONS:

  • None.
