Detailed Description

QUEST: Kill Dhohr Nuggetcutter

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Zogfang

STEPS:

  • Zogfang asks you to kill remaining dwarves from area
  • You go kill Dhohr Nuggetcutter and you get the reward from Zogfang

REWARD:

  • mithril nugget
  • 4000 XP
  • 35 karma in total

REPETITIONS:

  • after 14 days.
