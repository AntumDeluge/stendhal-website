Detailed Description

QUEST: Meet Santa anywhere around the World.

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Santa Claus

STEPS:

  • Find Santa
  • Say hi
  • Get reward
  • Get hat

REWARD:

  • a stocking which can be opened to obtain a random good reward: food, money, potions, items, etc...

REPETITIONS:None
